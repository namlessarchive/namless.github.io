  • Be prepared for an adventure

    The campaign aims to be about 5-9 sessions in length, a full-scale campaign but not a massive long term one. Team-comp is not required, feel free to pick any class and Humblewood race. Homebrew is case by case unless it is a Humblewood supplement, any offical Humblewood supplements can be used as long as I have access to their rules. Any other homebrew can just be shown to me for approval, but keep in mind the "saving nature" theme of the game.

  • For New Players

    The campaign will acomadate brand-new players, but some light learning is recomended. There are plenty of helpful 5E resources for new players on YouTube, but I'll be able to walk you through most of the game. We are using the SRD or '2014' 5E rules, so no feats at level 1 and actually important races. Ability score increase or feats is up to you, milestone leveling.

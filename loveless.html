Loveless
A Nameless Dating Simulator

The Romance Minigame

Loveless, is the name of a Visual Novel Dating Simulator that stars the characters of Nameless. Made with RenPy, the game provides players a short story with many choices and routes to pursue the cast of Nameless, and by extent the spinoff campaign Loveless. Not to be confused by the Dating Sim of the same name.

Release

At this time, the Loveless, A Nameless Dating Simulator project is in early development. The game will likely never be released. Any hope for this project died just like the "countless" loveless sessions that were set to happen.
